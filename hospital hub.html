Where to find Hospital

To find hospital go to the county search bar. Search according o the county and a number of hospitals will be listed.Choose the hospital of your desire.

How to book an Hospital

After searching the Hospital and selecting.Boooking is done using the Book button.Depending on the schedule that you have. You will be asked to input signs and symptoms,the way you feel,your email address,your phone number, date and time that you would like to the physician .

Feedback

The hospital will give you feedback after a few minutes with the services according to your signs and symptoms, time and date. You will be given further directions e.g the Doctor you will see and which room.

Emergency services

Just click emergency button and the location and a nearby hospital will be allocated to you.
